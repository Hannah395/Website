About Daniel Ricciardo's Racing Life

Daniel Ricciardo lived in Australia all of his younger years with his Mum (Grace Pulitano) and his Dad (Giuseppe "Joe" Ricciardo). Daniel also has one sister named Michelle. Daniel started his racing journey when he was 9 years old as a member of the Tiger Kart Club and was enter in many races. In 2005 he was then entered in the Western Australia Formula Ford championship. But because his car was aging it was uncompetitive and he finsihed 16th and retired 17th. Daniel was more successful in karts and was crowned as the Australia champion. In 2007 Daniel was then slected by the Red bull Junior Team. In the middle of the 2008 season daniel made his Formula 3 debut and joined Formula 3 Euro Series team. For the 2010 season Daniel signed with Tech 1 with some minor crashes along the way. Daniel first got into Formula 1 as a test and reserve driver. His very first Formula 1 team was Hispania Racing his last team was Racing Bulls.

Daniel Ricciardo has girlfriend, her name his Heidi Berger and they have been in a relationship since 2022. Daniel Ricciardo now has a farm in Western Australia.He enjoys his time outside riding motorbikes and looking after his animals. Daniel also owns a 5 bedroom house in Los Angeles. When hes in the U.S.A he uses this house as a base.
