Daniel Ricciardo's Achievements

Daniel Ricciardo has had 8 wins, 32 podiums and 3 pole positions. He is knowen for his time at Redbull, where he achieved his highest amount of pole positons. Daniel Ricciardo achieved third place finish in the drivers championship twice during his time at Redbull. But he has never won a championship in his Formula One career. During the very end of his season Daniel was to winning at all so this is why he got replaced.

During Daniels Formula one. Daniel drove for 14 seasons from 2011 to 2024. During this time he won 8 times these were 2014 Canadian Grand Prix, 2014 Hungarian Grand Prix, 2014 Belgain Grand Prix, 2016 Malaysian Grand Prix,2017 Azerbaijan Grand Prix,2018 Chinese Grand Prix, 2018 Monaco Grand Prix and 2021 Italian Grand Prix.
